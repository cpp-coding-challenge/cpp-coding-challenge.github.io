About

Cal Poly Pomona will be hosting a coding competition for high school students within the Pomona Unified School District. Each competitor will be given six problems and two hours, how many can you solve?

Experience

Get hands on experience working on real coding questions in a fun and fast paced environment

Prizes

Win cool prizes and the envy of your peers

Food

Of course food will be provided and who doesn't love pizza?

Schedule

The event will start at 11:00 on April 23 at the Cal Poly Pomona Campus

Sign In

We will open the sign in process at 11:00 AM and close the doors at 11:30 AM so be sure to show up on time!

Orientation

From 11:45 AM to 12:45 PM we will explain the rules and show you how to complete a problem and walk you through a practice submission.

Lunch

After orientation we will take an hour to enjoy the eighth wonder of the world that is Pizza.

Award Ceremony

Yayyy, you made it! From 4:15 PM to 4:45 PM we will announce the winners and distribute the prizes!

Example Problems

Try problems to stretch your skills and prepare for the competition

Largest Value Search

Create a program which asks the user for ten numbers. Print the max, min, and mean of the numbers.

Anagram

Write a program which takes two words and determines if the words are anagrams.

Factorial Calculator

Design a program that calculates the factorial of a user's numerical input.

Palindromes

Write a program which confirms whether a input string is a palindrome.
